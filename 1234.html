Problem Restatement

Rule/Formula Citation

To analyze the cost structures and profit decline, we will examine the unit cost details provided in the "COST ANALYSIS" section for each market and the overall average. Specifically, we will focus on:

  • 业务单位成本 (Business Unit Cost): Represents the direct costs associated with producing each unit.
  • 营业费用单位成本 (Operating Expense Unit Cost): Includes marketing and freight costs per unit.
  • 管理及财务费用单位成本 (Management and Financial Expense Unit Cost): Covers R&D, raw material holding costs, administrative expenses, and interest expenses per unit.
  • 总单位成本 (Total Unit Cost): The sum of all unit costs.
  • 税前净利 EBT (%) (Earnings Before Tax Margin): Indicates the profitability per unit of sales.
